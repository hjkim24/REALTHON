[강의요목]
딥러닝 기초에 대한 이해를 기반으로 최신 딥러닝 모델과 이론 및 구현, 그리고 최신 연구 동향을 학습한다. Diffusion model, Meta-learning, Foundation models(LLM, VLM, SAM), Agentic AI 등을 다룬다.

[주차별 강의 내용]
1주: Deep Learning Recap (ML Basics, CNN, RNN, Transformer)
2주: Retrieval (Information retrieval, Multimodal retrieval)
3주: Multimodal Learning (VQA, Image captioning, visual grounding)
4주: Weakly Supervised Learning (few-shot, zero-shot)
5주: Self-supervision and Pretraining in NLP (Word2Vec, BERT, GPT)
6주: Self-supervision and Pretraining in Computer Vision (MAE, CLIP)
7주: Generative Model 1 (VAE)
8주: Generative Model 2 (GAN)
9주: Generative Model 3 (Diffusion models)
10-11주: Diffusion Models and Their Applications / Discrete Diffusion Models
12주: Meta Learning
13주: Model Compression (Pruning, Distillation)
14주: Foundation models (LLM, VLM, SAM)
15주: Agentic AI (VLN, Mobile Agent, GOAT)
16주: Final Exam (기말고사)
